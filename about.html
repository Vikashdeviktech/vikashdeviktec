About Us

Mr Khalid.

For Indians wedding is the festival of love, relations and rituals. A happy marriage is the dream of every bride, groom and their family. Soft Planner is the event management company which is specialised in Planning Destination Weddings. Client satisfaction is our main objective. We plan each wedding event and gathering with our total involvement make your wedding is unique and memorable. Our team provides the best theme decorations for weddings, events and parties. We provide services like Venue Booking, Catering, DJ, tent and decorations. We also take birthday party bookings. Soft Planner is owned by Mr Khalid and based at Jharkhand.
